# Study Mate

> AI-powered study assistant that explains complex academic topics through the lens of a student's personal interests — making difficult content easier to understand and retain.

---

## Overview

Study Mate bridges the gap between difficult course content and student comprehension by leveraging AI to reframe academic concepts through familiar, interest-based analogies. Instead of generic textbook explanations, students receive personalised breakdowns tied to what they already love — whether that's basketball, dance, music, or any other domain.

---

## Aim

To improve academic comprehension and retention by making AI-generated explanations personal, relatable, and contextually meaningful for each student.

---

## Target Users

- University and high school students
- Self-learners tackling complex or unfamiliar topics
- Students who struggle with traditional, abstract academic explanations

---

## Features

- **Interest-Mapped Explanations** — AI explains any topic using analogies tied to the user's personal interests
- **Flashcard Generator** — Auto-generates flashcards from topics or pasted content, framed in the user's interest domain
- **Quiz Mode** — Short AI-generated quizzes to reinforce retention
- **Paste or Upload Content** — Accepts pasted text or uploaded PDFs for context-specific explanations
- **Simplicity Slider** — Control explanation complexity from beginner to advanced
- **Session History** — Revisit past explanations and topics
- **Bookmarks** — Save helpful explanations for later review
